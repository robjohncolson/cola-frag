\section{Preparation Instructions}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Prepare Dilutions} (if not already prepared):
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Patchoulol 10\%: 1 g Patchoulol + 9 g DPG, warm to 40--50°C if needed.
        \item Patchouli Absolute 10\%: 1 g Patchouli Absolute + 9 g DPG, warm to 40--50°C if needed.
        \item Black Currant Bud Absolute 10\%: 100 microliters absolute + 900 microliters IPM.
        \item Birch Tar 10\%: 100 microliters Birch Tar + 900 microliters DPG.
        \item Paradisamide 10\%: 100 microliters Paradisamide + 900 microliters DPG.
        \item 2-Isobutyl Quinoline 10\%: 100 microliters 2-IBQ + 900 microliters DPG.
        \item Damascone Alpha 10\%: 100 microliters Damascone Alpha + 900 microliters DPG.
        \item Galbanum Absolute 10\%: 100 microliters Galbanum Absolute + 900 microliters DPG.
        \item Iso E Super 10\%: 1 g Iso E Super + 9 g DPG.
        \item Petitgrain Absolute 10\%: 100 microliters Petitgrain Absolute + 900 microliters DPG.
        \item Clary Sage Absolute 23\%: 230 microliters Clary Sage Absolute + 770 microliters DPG.
        \item Galaxolide 23\%: 230 microliters Galaxolide + 770 microliters DPG.
        \item Boronia Absolute 10\%: 100 microliters Boronia Absolute + 900 microliters DPG.
    \end{itemize}
    
    \item Add 487 microliters Ethanol (95\%) to a 1 ml amber glass vial.
    
    \item Add ingredients in order using separate micropipette tips: Patchoulol 10\%, Patchouli Absolute 10\%, 3\% Tonkin Deer Musk Tincture, Iso E Super 10\%, Helvetolide, Ambroxan, Galaxolide 23\%, Veramoss 23\%, 2-Isobutyl Quinoline 10\%, Birch Tar 10\%, Black Currant Bud Absolute 10\%, Damascone Alpha 10\%, Clary Sage Absolute 23\%, Pink Pepper EO, Petitgrain Absolute 10\%, Linalool, Coranol, Lyral, Sweet Orange EO, Star Anise EO, Bergamot EO, Lemon EO, Neroli Oil, Pineapple Essence, Paradisamide 10\%, Galbanum Absolute 10\%, Atlas Cedarwood Absolute, Boronia Absolute 10\%, Hedione.
    
    \item Seal vial, roll gently for 1--2 minutes to blend.
    
    \item Rest for 48 hours in a cool, dark place.
    
    \item Test on skin, adjusting if needed: Deer Musk (20--40 microliters), Paradisamide 10\% (40--60 microliters), Veramoss 23\% (15--35 microliters), or Boronia Absolute 10\% (5--12 microliters).
\end{enumerate}

\section{Expected Scent Profile}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Top (5--30 min)}: Bright bergamot and lemon opening with pineapple, sweet orange, and neroli florals creating a sparkling citrus-floral bouquet. Pink pepper and star anise add spice, while galbanum provides green freshness. Boronia begins to introduce the signature tea-like quality.
    
    \item \textbf{Heart (30 min -- 3 hr)}: Petitgrain and clary sage with black currant tartness form the spicy-floral core. Atlas cedarwood adds dry-woody structure while florals (lyral, coranol) provide body. Boronia absolute develops the distinctive violet-powdery tea note characteristic of Aventus.
    
    \item \textbf{Base (3--8+ hr)}: Patchoulol, Patchouli Absolute, and Veramoss create the chypre foundation with earthy-mossy depth. Helvetolide, Ambroxan, and Deer Musk provide musky-ambergris richness, while Paradisamide adds clean-industrial edge. Atlas Cedarwood maintains dry-woody character throughout the drydown.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Performance}: 5--7 hours, moderate projection with improved brightness, tea-like complexity, and better balance between citrus freshness and masculine woody-chypre base.

\textbf{Key Improvements}: Enhanced citrus opening, signature tea note from Boronia, dry-woody lift from Atlas Cedarwood, and orange blossom bridge from Neroli to better integrate the composition.
